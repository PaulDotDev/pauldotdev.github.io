About

I build the whole thing, from the database schema to the pixels, and the infrastructure that keeps it running.

These days I'm building AI-enabled product and platform work at Cerebra, focused on reliable systems and end-to-end delivery. Before that, I spent years working across languages, industries, and codebases, building software that needed to hold up under real-world constraints.

My path in is different from most. I started as an electronics technician keeping mission-critical systems alive on offshore drilling rigs, where downtime has real consequences. That bar for reliability is how I build software now.
